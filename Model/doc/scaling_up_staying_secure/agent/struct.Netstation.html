Expand description

The Netstation agent. A level below Area and one above Household.

A Netstation is a power distribution house that is situated in a neighbourhood. They take the High Voltage of the grid and transform this to the known 230V. As this is the point where the voltage of the grid is determined, this agent is responsible for the Voltage stability monitoring of the grid.
